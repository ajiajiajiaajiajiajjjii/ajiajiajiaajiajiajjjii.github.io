Descrizione del bando
Titolo del progetto di ricerca in italiano PRIN 2022 - Expanding the toolbox for cereal breeding: high-throughput genomics, 2D-3D phenomics and artificial intelligence for breeding with increasing genome complexity, from barley to durum and bread wheat (POLYPLOIDBREEDING 4.0)
Titolo del progetto di ricerca in inglese PRIN 2022 - Expanding the toolbox for cereal breeding: high-throughput genomics, 2D-3D phenomics and artificial intelligence for breeding with increasing genome complexity, from barley to durum and bread wheat (POLYPLOIDBREEDING 4.0)
Area CUN 07 - Scienze agrarie e veterinarie
S.S.D AGR/07 - GENETICA AGRARIA
Descrizione sintetica in italiano Selezione pubblica, per titoli e colloquio, per il conferimento di un Assegno di ricerca Senior presso la sede di Milano dell’Istituto di Biologia e Biotecnologia Agraria del CNR nell’ambito del programma di ricerca a “Expanding the toolbox for cereal breeding: high-throughput genomics, 2D-3D phenomics and artificial intelligence for breeding with increasing genome complexity, from barley to durum and bread wheat (POLYPLOIDBREEDING 4.0)” - PRIN (Progetti di ricerca di Rilevante Interesse Nazionale) 2022 – D.D. n. 104 del 02-02-2022 - Macrosettore LS Life Sciences settore LS2 “Integrative Biology: from Genes and Genomes to Systems” - Codice 2022BACN8A, finanziato dall’Unione europea - Next Generation EU, per la tematica : “Metodi di machine learning ed intelligenza artificiale per l’agricoltura di precisione e la selezione genomica in specie con differente ploidia”, sotto la responsabilità scientifica del Dott. Filippo Biscarini.
Descrizione sintetica in inglese Public selection, based on qualifications and interview, for the assignment of no. 1 Senior Research Grant for conducting research at the Institute of Agricultural Biology and Biotechnology (CNR-IBBA) in Milano, within the project PRIN 2022 “Expanding the toolbox for cereal breeding: high-throughput genomics, 2D-3D phenomics and artificial intelligence for breeding with increasing genome complexity, from barley to durum and bread wheat (POLYPLOIDBREEDING 4.0)”, funded by NextGenerationEU - European Union, on the topic “Machine-learning and neural-networks methods for precision agriculture and genomic selection in species with different ploidy”, under the scientific responsability of Dr. Filippo Biscarini
